History of the company

Fino Dn'M is a company founded in 2020 by two friends in Argentina chasing the dream of gourmet food at an impressingly low cost, manteining both quality of ingredients and manufacturing, this has been posible by in house sourcing all the ingredients like tomatoes, carrots, onions, lettuce, avocados, oranges, apples, bananas, strawberries, blueberries and more, making this way cheaper and natural than other alternatives.
